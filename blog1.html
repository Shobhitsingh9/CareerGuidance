Top 10 Commerce Colleges in India – Eligibility and Placements

Most people in India assume that the Commerce stream falls in between Science and Arts. People feel that there are fewer prospects here than in Science, which is a common misconception. There are endless opportunities in the Commerce domain, and it provides students with a wealth of career options to explore and grow in. A further common misperception among students is that commerce with maths brings up a large range of career opportunities, but commerce without maths offers limited alternatives. However, commerce without maths will not limit their career options in any way. Once a student has started to follow commerce and is in the process of selecting colleges, there are a few things to keep in mind, like: Eligibility, Entrance exams, the Curriculum, College Placements, and the Alumni. So here are the top 10 best commerce colleges in India that provide high-quality education and prepare students for a variety of professional degrees and job opportunities in the corporate and industrial sectors, such as CA (chartered accounts), CS, Audit officers, Bankers, Stock Brokers, Wealth Managers, Data Analysts, and so on.

1. Shri Ram College of Commerce, Delhi

Every Commerce student has at least once dreamt of going to Shri Ram College of Commerce. While many of us believe that SRCC only offers two courses- B.com (Hons) & Economics (Hons.), you will be surprised to learn that the college offers many more! In addition to the two undergraduate degrees, SRCC provides a variety of postgraduate degrees, including M.Com, M.A. in Economics, MBA, and Postgraduate Diploma in Global Business Operations (GBO).
Eligibility: Admission to the undergraduate (UG) programs of Shri Ram College of Commerce is merit based that means a candidate must have an aggregate of 45% of marks in the qualifying examinations.
Placements: Many companies seek to hire students from SRCC because of the renowned alumni such as Late Sh. Arun Jaitely, Mr. Rajat Sharma, Justice Rohinton Fali Nariman, Mr. Ravi Memani and others. Google, ICICI Bank, Barclays, Ernst & Young, Deloitte, Zomato, and KPMG are just a few of the top industry names who participated in the placement process.

2. Hindu College, Delhi

Hindu College is one of the oldest colleges of the University of Delhi. For commerce students, the college offers a B.Com (Hons) and an M.Com.
Eligibility: College admissions have been based on either an entrance exam or a merit-based selection process (or both in some cases). The pre – requisite for B.Com admission is to complete 10+2 with a specific set of required subjects, and based on the cut-off.
Placements: Companies like DE Shaw, Deloitte, Ernst & Young, HDFC Bank, India Bulls, Jaypee Group, KPMG, Maruti Suzuki have hired students from Hindu college. Arnab Goswami, Kavita Ramdas, Samir Modi, Vinod Kumar Khanna, and many other notable alumni have attended Hindu College, making it more prestigious.

3. Lady Shri Ram College for Women, Delhi

Lady Shri Ram College (LSR) is an all-women’s college affiliated to Delhi University. It is one of the few colleges recognised by the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD). LSR delivers 25 courses in total, including all UG, PG, and certificate programs. The college offers B.com(Hons) and B.A in Economics for commerce students.
Eligibility: A 12th grade student must have a cumulative grade point average of 45 percent and Math/Business as compulsory subjects in order to be admitted.
Placements: American Express, DE Shaw, Deloitte, Grofers, JSW Steel, KPMG, McKinsey, NIIT, PWC, and Samsung are among the companies actively involved in the college’s placement process.This prestigious college has produced graduates such as Vinita Bali, Meenakshi Gopinath, Naina Lal Kidwai, Gita Gopinath, and others.

4. Loyola College, Chennai

Loyola College in Chennai is one of India’s most elite colleges. It is a self-governing institution connected with Madras University. The college offers not only undergraduate and postgraduate degrees but also M. Phil and 14 doctoral degrees in a plethora of fields like humanities, science, and commerce. B.Com (General), B.Com (CS), and B.B.A. are the most popular programmes for commerce students at the college.
Eligibility: Admittance to the college’s undergraduate programmes is highly dependent on the performance of the qualifying examination in 12th grade.
Placements: The college affirms an active Training and Placement unit that helps students in their final years to find employment. More than 100 organisations have hired Loyola graduates, including Amazon, TCS, Wipro, Stratagem Solutions, Syntel, and others.

5. Christ University, Bangalore

The college offers undergraduate degrees like BBA with specialisation in Business Analytics/Finance/International Business, B.Com, B.Com(Hons) and postgraduate degrees in M.Com, MBA and PGDM.The bulk of Christ University’s courses are admitted through the CUET (Christ University Entrance Test), and applicants must meet Christ University cutoffs to be considered for the course in which they are interested.
Eligibility: A minimum of 55 percent in any stream (Humanities, Social Sciences, Commerce & Management, and Sciences) from any recognised board in India in 12th grade(Karnataka PUC / ISC / CBSE / NIOS / State Boards).
Placements: Christ University Bangalore’s placement unit assists students in obtaining internships, live projects, and summer and winter placements. DE Shaw & Co., Arcesium, Axxela, and Bain Capability Network were among the top recruitment firms that hired Christ University students

6. Narsee Monjee College of Commerce and Economics, Mumbai

Narsee Monjee College of Commerce & Economics is a leading college affiliated to Mumbai University. The college organizes regular industrial visits to give students hands-on experience with how a business operates. NPAT (National Test for Programs after Twelfth) is the official entrance test for admissions to Undergraduate Degree and Integrated Degree Programs such as BBA, B.Com Hons, B.Sc. Finance, and B.Sc. Economics at the college.
Eligibility: A candidate must have passed 10+2 or equivalent examination and must have obtained a minimum of 60% aggregate marks for being eligible to BBA/B.Sc. Finance & B. Com (Hons) program with Mathematics.
Placements: Some of the companies that hire college students include Citigroup, Honeywell, TCS, Federal Bank, Ernst and Young, and KPMG

7. Hansraj College, Delhi

Shahrukh Khan, the king of hearts, is a famous alumnus of Hansraj College. Apart from that, it is the most well-known college on the North Campus, offering courses in Liberal Arts, Science, and Commerce. The college offers more than 34 courses across five different streams that include B.Com (Hons), B.A Economics and M.Com for commerce students. Aside from these degrees, the college offers certificate and diploma programmes in a variety of other subjects.
Eligibility: A candidate must have a 45 percent or higher aggregate in English/Hindi, as well as a combination of the best three subjects from Mathematics, Accountancy, Economics, and Business Studies/Commerce.
Placement: The college’s placement cell works hard, and as a result, they have increased placements from a variety of leading firms across a diverse range of companies, including McKinsey & Company, Bain Capability Network, Boston Consulting Group, United Airlines, and DeShaw.

8. Mithibai College of Arts, Chauhan Institute of Science & Amrutben Jivanlal College of Commerce and Economics, Pune

Mithibai College of Arts, Chauhan Institute of Science, and Amrutben Jivanlal College of Commerce and Economics furnish education in the arts, science, and commerce, respectively.
The college offers a plethora of courses in undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in multiple specializations. B.Com (General), B.Com in Accounting and Finance/ Financial Markets, B.Com(Hons), B.M.S and M.Com are highly popular courses among commerce students.
Eligibility: To be eligible for admission to the college, a candidate must have passed the 10+2 or equivalent examination and obtained a minimum of 60% aggregate marks. Placement: The college’s training and placement cell secures lucrative placement opportunities for students and offers full-time/part-time jobs as well as internships. Aside from the final placement drive, the cell encourages students to improve their professional skills through training programs. Morgan Stanley, Future First, Tech Mahindra, and Nokia are among the college’s recruiting partners.

9. Madras Christian College, Chennai

Madras Christian College [MCC] is a well-known and prestigious higher education institution that provides excellence in the arts and sciences. The college’s notable alumni network includes civil servants, educators, business people, administrators, and political leaders from all over the world.
Eligibility: It is critical for students seeking admission to Madras Christian College to meet the eligibility criteria that have been separately defined for B.Com (General), B.Com in Accounting/Finance, and perhaps a candidate must have passed 10 + 2 with a minimum aggregate of 50%.
Placement: Internships are available to Madras Christian College students who want to obtain practical experience. They have been engaged by top businesses like Zoho, Infosys, Wipro, Deloitte, Accenture, TCS, and many others.

10. Ramjas College, Delhi

Ramjas is one of the first three institutions founded by the University of Delhi, and it is now considered one of the best in the country. Thousands of students aspire to study at Ramjas, but only the best of them are able to make the most of their opportunity.
Eligibility: In order to gain entry to B.Com (Hons), B.Com, or B.A Economics, students must have a minimum aggregate of 45 percent in the qualifying examination and 50 percent in Mathematics.
Placements: The placement cell at the college organises seminars and workshops for students, along with the placement drive. Deloitte, Excel Pack Limited, Wipro, Genpact, NDTV, The Oberoi Group, Right Relevance, Grofers.com, Google, Dell, The Financial Express, ICICI Prudential Life Insurance, and Barclays are among the top hiring firms.
